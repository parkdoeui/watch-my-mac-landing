How it works

Three steps. Under a minute.

1

Lock it

Click the WatchMyMac icon in your menu bar. Click Lock My Mac and scan the QR code to start recording. No setup, no login, no configuration is needed.

2

Walk away

Your phone will open the stream viewer instantly in your browser. Watch your surroundings of your Mac while away. Make alarm sound to alert if someone suspiciously approach your Mac.

3

Unlock your laptop on return

Unlock when you return. Click on unlock button from the stream viewer. Your session recording is saved locally on your Mac.

FAQ

macOS blocks apps that aren't from the App Store by default. After downloading, right-click (or Control-click) the app and choose Open — then click Open again in the dialog. You only need to do this once. If you don't see that option, go to System Settings → Privacy & Security, scroll down, and click Open Anyway next to the WatchMyMac entry.
